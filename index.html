Questions we get a lot

How long does a typical site take?

Most small business sites launch in 2–3 weeks once we approve the concept and have your content (logo, photos, hours, services).

What do you need from me to start?

Brand name, contact info, service list, a few photos, and what action you want customers to take (call, text, book, directions). We’ll guide you step by step.

Can you write the copy?

Yes. We’ll draft concise, plain-English copy that answers common questions and drives action. You’ll review and approve before launch.

Do you handle domains, hosting, and security?

Yep—domain setup, SSL, hosting, backups, and small updates can be handled through our care plans.

Will my site show up on Google?

We use simple on-page SEO and polish your Google Business Profile so local customers can actually find you. Big ranking jumps depend on competition and content, but we set you up right.
